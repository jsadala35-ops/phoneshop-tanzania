# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, users, and community members of the PhoneShop Pro Edition project. We value diversity and respect for all individuals, regardless of background, identity, or experience.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

- **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with courtesy and kindness
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- **Be collaborative** - Work together to solve problems and advance the project
- **Be professional** - Keep discussions focused on the project and community goals
- **Give credit** - Acknowledge and appreciate contributions from others
- **Be patient** - Remember that people have different skill levels and perspectives
- **Listen actively** - Seek to understand before being understood
- **Report issues** - Bring concerns to the attention of maintainers

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment** - Any form of harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- **Discrimination** - Discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, disability, age, religion, or any other protected characteristic
- **Abuse** - Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language
- **Trolling** - Intentionally disruptive or inflammatory behavior
- **Spam** - Unsolicited promotional content or repeated irrelevant messages
- **Privacy violations** - Sharing private information without consent
- **Illegal activity** - Any behavior that violates local laws or regulations
- **Disruptive behavior** - Derailing discussions or creating hostile environments

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All spaces within the GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project-related communications (email, chat, forums)
- Project events and meetups (both online and in-person)
- Social media when discussing the project

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers:

**Report Options:**

1. **Email**: jsadala35@gmail.com
2. **GitHub Issue**: Create a private security advisory
3. **Phone**: +255 673 409 790 (urgent matters only)

When reporting, please include:
- Your contact information
- Description of the incident
- Names of involved parties (if known)
- Dates and times
- Any evidence or screenshots
- Your preferred resolution

### Investigation

All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly. We will:

1. Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. Investigate the incident thoroughly
3. Keep the reporter informed of progress
4. Maintain confidentiality of all parties involved
5. Take appropriate action based on findings

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **Warning** - Formal notice of violation
2. **Temporary Ban** - Suspension from project participation (7-30 days)
3. **Permanent Ban** - Removal from the project and community
4. **Legal Action** - In cases of illegal activity or severe harassment

The severity of the response will match the severity of the violation.

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was made in error, you may appeal by:

1. Sending an email to jsadala35@gmail.com with "APPEAL" in the subject line
2. Providing detailed explanation of your appeal
3. Requesting a review by the project maintainers

Appeals will be reviewed within 7 days.

## Confidentiality

- **Reporters** remain confidential unless they choose to disclose their identity
- **Accused parties** will be informed of the allegations and given opportunity to respond
- **Resolution details** are kept confidential to protect all parties

## Continuous Improvement

This Code of Conduct is a living document. We welcome feedback and suggestions for improvement. If you have ideas for making our community more inclusive and respectful, please share them.
